The Best Movies According to Suswagatam

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Godfather

I liked the character of Don Vito Corleone the most.

The Pianist

I loved the scene where the protagonist was imitating playing the piano with his fingers in the air.

Manchester by the sea

This movie has my heart. The portrayal of sorrow, grief, guilt, and everything inbetween is immaculate.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

No words. Watch it.

Midnight in Paris

A heaven for a dreamer like me. I wish it had happened with me.
